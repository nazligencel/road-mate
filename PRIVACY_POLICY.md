# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 17, 2026**

Road Mate ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Road Mate mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Name and email address
- Password (stored in hashed form)
- Google account information (if you sign in with Google)

### 1.2 Profile Information
You may voluntarily provide:
- Profile photo and bio
- Tagline and current travel route
- Vehicle details (type, brand, model, and photos)
- Photo gallery images

### 1.3 Location Data
With your permission, we collect precise GPS location data to:
- Display nearby nomads on the map
- Enable the SOS alert system
- Show your location to other users
- Provide nearby places (mechanics, fuel stations, markets)

You can disable location services at any time through the App settings or your device settings.

### 1.4 Messages and Communications
We collect and store:
- Direct messages between users
- Discussion posts and comments in the community forum
- Assist request messages
- AI Assistant conversation history (Pro subscribers)

### 1.5 Subscription and Purchase Data
If you subscribe to Road Mate Pro, we collect:
- Subscription status (free or Pro)
- Purchase transaction data processed through RevenueCat

### 1.6 Device Information
We automatically collect:
- Device type and operating system
- Push notification token (for delivering notifications)
- App version

### 1.7 Usage Data
We may collect information about how you interact with the App, including:
- Features accessed and actions performed
- Connections, blocked users, and meeting requests
- Activities created or joined

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide Core Services**: Operate the App, manage your account, and deliver the features you use (messaging, map, community, connections).
- **Enable Social Features**: Display your profile to other users, show nearby nomads on the map, and facilitate connections and meeting requests.
- **Deliver Notifications**: Send push notifications for SOS alerts, messages, friend requests, meeting requests, and community activity.
- **Process Subscriptions**: Manage your Pro subscription status and verify purchases.
- **Provide Emergency Assistance**: Distribute SOS alerts to nearby users within a 100 km radius when activated.
- **Improve the App**: Understand usage patterns to enhance features and user experience.
- **Ensure Safety**: Enforce our blocking system and community guidelines.

---

## 3. Information Shared with Other Users

Road Mate is a social platform for nomads. The following information is visible to other users:

- Your **profile information** (name, bio, tagline, route, vehicle details, photos, and gallery)
- Your **approximate location** on the map (when location services are enabled)
- Your **discussion posts and comments** in the community forum
- Your **activities and events** you create or join
- Your **SOS alert status and location** (when SOS is activated, visible to users within 100 km)

Direct messages are only visible to the sender and recipient.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process your data:

### 4.1 Google Services
- **Google Sign-In**: For account authentication. Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Google Maps & Places API**: For map display, location search, and nearby places. Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 4.2 RevenueCat
We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase data in accordance with their [Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

### 4.3 Expo Push Notifications
We use Expo's push notification service to deliver notifications to your device. Subject to [Expo's Privacy Policy](https://expo.dev/privacy).

### 4.4 Open-Meteo
We use Open-Meteo to display weather information based on your location. Open-Meteo is a free, open-source weather API.

### 4.5 Hosting and Infrastructure
Our backend services are hosted on Railway, and our database is hosted on Neon (PostgreSQL). Your data is stored securely on these platforms.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained as long as your account is active.
- **Messages**: Stored on our servers for the duration of your account.
- **SOS Alerts**: Automatically expire and are deactivated after 2 hours.
- **Location Data**: Your current location is updated in real time and is not stored as historical location data.
- **Photos and Gallery**: Retained until you delete them or delete your account.
- **Assist Requests**: Retained until resolved or deleted by the user.

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

- Encrypted data transmission over HTTPS
- Hashed password storage
- Token-based authentication (JWT)
- API keys stored securely as environment variables

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View your personal data through your profile settings.
- **Update**: Edit your profile information, photos, and preferences at any time.
- **Delete Content**: Remove your photos, gallery images, discussions, and assist requests.
- **Block Users**: Block other users to prevent them from seeing your profile or contacting you.
- **Disable Location**: Turn off location services through the App or device settings.
- **Manage Notifications**: Enable or disable push notifications in the App settings.
- **Delete Account**: Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.
